About UberFix

Built for Maintenance & Operations

UberFix is a B2B maintenance platform built for retail chains, F&B brands, and multi-branch businesses. It centralizes all maintenance requests, branch data, and technician workflows in one place, so every issue is tracked from the first report to final resolution.

From incident reporting and technician dispatching to approvals, visits, and maintenance history, UberFix gives operations teams full visibility on what is happening in every location, every day.
